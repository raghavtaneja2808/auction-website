OVER 70 YEARS OF TRUST

Fortunes: where tradition meets modern bidding

founded in 1942

Established by two brothers, Richard and William Hart, Fortunes began as a modest auction house in 1942, specializing in rare art, collectibles, and valuable heirlooms. With a passion for preserving heritage and connecting collectors with unique finds, Fortunes quickly became a trusted name among collectors and investors. Throughout the 1950s and 60s, the Harts expanded operations, opening offices in bustling cities from London to New York, helping people rediscover and cherish the value of the past.

to the digital age

In the early 2000s, with changing technology and market dynamics, the third generation of the Hart family, Adam and Peter, took the helm. Determined to uphold their grandfather’s vision, they embraced the digital era, transforming Fortunes from a beloved auction house into a fully accessible online platform. As Fortunes evolved, the platform grew to become a premier destination for collectors worldwide, while still honoring the elegance and integrity of its original physical auction roots.

a legacy of trust & excellence

Every piece auctioned at Fortunes undergoes meticulous verification, a practice dating back to Richard and William’s era. Our expert curators and appraisal teams, with decades of combined experience, ensure that Fortunes continues to offer only the most valued and authentic artifacts, from vintage jewelry to renowned artworks and historical memorabilia.

Today, Fortunes represents a perfect blend of tradition and innovation. Our online auction rooms allow for live, real-time bidding, keeping the excitement of the auction house alive while connecting a global community of collectors and sellers. With offices in Los Angeles, London, and Hong Kong, and a specialized appraisal team in Paris, Fortunes remains dedicated to excellence in both its technology and personal service, with each auction item treated as a piece of history.
